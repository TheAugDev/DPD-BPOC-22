Our Commitment

"Service Before Self"

We, the members of BPOC #22, have completed our rigorous training and are proud to begin our service with the Denton Police Department. This journey has tested our limits and forged a bond of camaraderie and shared purpose. We are dedicated to protecting and serving the community of Denton with integrity, courage, and compassion.

We are also proud to have trained alongside our colleagues from other distinguished agencies, including officers from UNT Police, Aubrey PD, and the Denton Fire Department. This multi-agency class strengthened our bonds and our shared commitment to serving our respective communities.
